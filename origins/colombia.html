Colombia

Colombian green coffee - balanced, bright, consistently excellent

Colombia is Canada's most-sourced specialty origin for good reason. Diverse microclimates across Huila, Nariño, Tolima, Quindío, Cundinamarca, and Antioquia produce consistently clean, sweet, and well-structured coffees that excel in every brew method.

About our Colombia green coffee selection

We import Colombian Excelso EP, Excelso GP, organic, Rainforest Alliance, and Women's Producer lots - including our Terra Rosa Women's Lot from Huila and EcoTerra Women's Lot from Nariño. Whether you need a dependable blend base or a distinctive single-origin, our Colombian catalogue has you covered.

Flavour profile

Expect milk chocolate, caramel, red apple, peach, honey, citrus, and brown sugar.
