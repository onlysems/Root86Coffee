Who We Are

Canada's green coffee community, rooted in relationships

Root 86 Coffee was built for the roasters. The small-batch craftspeople who demand quality at every step. We source, import, and deliver exceptional green coffee to micro-roasters across Canada, handling the complexity of international trade so you can focus on what you do best.

Proudly Canadian and proudly independent, our three warehouse network means faster delivery and lower freight costs, no matter where in Canada you are roasting.

I

Canadian Sourcing Network

Three strategically located warehouses in Vancouver, Levis, and Parksville keep freight costs low and delivery times fast for roasters coast to coast.

II

Origin Transparency

We tell you exactly where your coffee comes from. Region, producer, process, altitude. Every lot is documented so you can tell the full story to your customers.

III

Ethical Lots First

From Organic and Fair Trade to Women's Producer cooperatives, we actively source and prioritise certifications that make a measurable difference at origin.

IV

Roaster-First Service

No automated runaround. Real people respond to every quote. Container consolidation, quality control, and sample programs available to all roasteries.
