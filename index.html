Who We Are

Canada's green coffee community, rooted in relationships

Root 86 Coffee was built for the roasters — the small-batch craftspeople who demand quality at every step. We source, import, and deliver exceptional green coffee to micro-roasters across Canada, handling the complexity of international trade so you can focus on what you do best.

Our three warehouse network — in Vancouver, Lévis, and Parksville — means faster delivery and lower freight costs, no matter where in Canada you're roasting.

  • Dedicated green coffee brokers with deep origin knowledge
  • Transparent sourcing — know where your coffee comes from
  • Container consolidation for smaller roasteries
  • Quality control services and sample programs
  • Proudly Canadian, proudly supporting micro-roasters
